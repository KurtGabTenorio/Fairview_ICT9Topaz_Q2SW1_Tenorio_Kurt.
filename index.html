Courage the Cowardly Dog Characters


Cowardly Dog Characters Design of each Character
Courage the Dog - A dog that is often frightened of things even though his name is courage. He is always spooked by Eustace since Eustace doesn't like Courage cause he easily gets frightened. But where Muriel & Eustace live is a place where Courage's nightmares occur.
Muriel - She is the kindest old lady you would ever see in this show. She adopted Courage because she saw him being upset by something. As Muriels personality, she takes good care of Courage because of him getting frightened.
Eustace - He is a grumpy old man who often bullies Courage the Dog due to Courages lack of strength to fight scary things. Eustace always makes fun of Courage because of him getting easily scared by anomaly things. He also mocks him and his name due to Courage having the lack of courage to fight his own weaknesses
